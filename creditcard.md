Loompay offers secured credit cards that let you spend crypto as fiat. All cards are denominated in USD.

**How to issue a card:**

The Loompay Secured Card is issued seamlessly through the **Loompay.io platform** with a fully automated, on-chain system.

Upon sign-up or wallet connection, each user receives a **pre-issued card and wallet**—ready to activate in seconds.

### **1. Issuance Flow**

1. The user signs in via **wallet** or **email**.
2. Upon login, **Loompay automatically creates a secure wallet** and a **pre-issued card profile** for the user.
3. The virtual card is **automatically activated** with an initial **monthly spending limit of $2,000**.
4. After successful **KYC verification**, the card’s **spending limits are instantly upgraded**, enabling full global usage.

---

### **Card Types**

- **Virtual Card:** Instantly issued and activated after login. It can be used for **online payments** or added to **mobile wallets** for contactless transactions.
- **Physical Card:** Available **after KYC verification** and shipped within **5–10 business days**. Ideal for **in-store purchases** and **ATM withdrawals**.

## **3. KYC (Know Your Customer)**

Loompay uses a **tiered verification system** that maintains compliance while optimizing the user experience.

KYC is only triggered when users choose to **activate their Physical Card** or upgrade **their spending limits**, ensuring a seamless and low-friction onboarding experience.

| Level | Verification Requirement | Daily Limit | Features Unlocked |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Level 0 (Pre-Issued)** | Email + Wallet login | $2,000/month | Pre-issued card, USDT or crypto deposit. Activate virtual card, fiat transactions.  |
| **Level 1** | ID (National/Passport). Advanced (Address Proof + Selfie) | $10,000 - Unlimited/day | Physical card, ATM withdrawals, staking |

**Highlights:**

- AI-based verification with under 60-second approval.
- ISO/IEC 27001 certified identity provider.
- Automatic tier transitions as user activity grows.
